The World of HueMe

The chance to view a world of disconnect as an opportunity is at your fingertips. The world of HueMe combines colour theory and compassion. To use the words of Dr Amanda Pike of the Florida Art Therapy Association: “Colour has a profound effect on our lives. Colour is a fundamental aspect of human perception, and it influences cognition and mood”. Through anonimity, individuals can feel a sense of privacy and safety. These pathways also encourage expression without censure, but nurture respect. A community is waiting to hear about your day.
